Returns & Refunds

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with your order, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

How to Initiate a Return

1. Log in to your account and go to "Order History".

2. Select the order you want to return and click "Request Return".

3. Pack the item securely in its original packaging.

4. Drop off the package at any authorized shipping location.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days. The refund will be credited back to your original payment method.
